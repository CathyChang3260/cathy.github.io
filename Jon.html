Jon Arbuckle:

Jon Arbuckle is Garfield's fun and clumsy owner, who gets pranked and beaten by his pet cat. In the beginning years, Jon Arbuckle lived with his friend Lyman, who owned a dog Odie, but later owned Odie. Also, his appearance had a long face and skinny neck, but changed into a chubby-cheeked man.
