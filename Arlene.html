Arlene

Arlene is Garfield's girlfriend who is a pink alley cat. For some unknown reason, she was very hostile towards Garfield, but then she starts dating him. She also has buck teeth with a very big space in between. Garfield and Arlene always fight because of Arlene's eating habits or Garfield's vanity.
