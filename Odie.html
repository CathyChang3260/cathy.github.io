Odie:

Odie is a fun and loving dog, even if he can drool all over the place, make fun of Garfield, and still come out as the victim. He was based off a car dealership ad, which included Odie the Idiot. At first, Odie was a dog with black ears, long neck, and big head.

Later, he evolved into a dog with brown ears and long mouth, instead of black ears with a very long neck.
